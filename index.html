Badlion but better.

The Badlion Modding group has been dedicated to improving the original Badlion client. By utilizing the fabric mod loader you can load additional mods to enhance your Minecraft experience that aren't included in the base Badlion such as ChromaHud and Surely, Not, etc Enough Items. It also has all of the hard to audit native libraries removed so it is better for privacy as you can see exactly what it is doing and supports all operating systems and launchers unlike the original.
